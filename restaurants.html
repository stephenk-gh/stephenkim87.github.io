    The Barbeque Pit

    The Barbeque Pit down at 2509 E Cherry St Seattle, WA 98122 is a great pick if you're hungry for meat.

    The pulled pork sandwiches are excellent, and the ribs are fantastic.

    We heartily recommend heading down there and giving them a try yourselves.

    Piroshky Piroshky

    Piroshky Piroshky is wonderful if you're looking for sweet pastries -- or even more meat.

    You can hit up the bakery at 1908 Pike Place, Seattle, WA 98101.

    Get there early if you want them fresh, though -- they're gone quick!
